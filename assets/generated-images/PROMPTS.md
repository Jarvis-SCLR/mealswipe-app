# Recipe Image Prompts (V9 - Approved)

## Style Guidelines
- **Extreme close-up** filling the entire frame
- **Home kitchen settings** (not restaurant/commercial)
- **Regular home dishes** (dinner plates, mixing bowls, frying pans)
- **Natural/warm lighting** (window light, evening indoor, cozy)
- **Vertical portrait orientation, 9:16 aspect ratio**
