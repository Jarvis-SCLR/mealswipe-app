How do I save a recipe?
Simply swipe right on any recipe card you like! The recipe will automatically be saved to your menu. You can also tap the heart icon on any recipe to save it.
How do I create a meal plan?
Go to the "Plan" tab at the bottom of the app. You'll see your weekly calendar. Tap on any day to add a meal from your saved recipes, or drag and drop recipes from your saved list.
How does the grocery list work?
Once you've added meals to your weekly plan, tap "Generate Grocery List" on the Plan tab. MealSwipe automatically combines ingredients from all your planned meals into a single shopping list, organized by category.

üí≥ Premium & Billing

What's included in Premium?
Premium includes unlimited saved recipes, family meal voting, AI-powered meal suggestions, nutrition tracking, meal reminders, and the ability to add your own custom recipes. Free users can save up to 20 recipes.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can manage your subscription anytime through your App Store settings. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > MealSwipe and tap "Cancel Subscription". You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.
Can I get a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple. You can request a refund through reportaproblem.apple.com within 14 days of purchase.
How do I restore my purchases?
On the Premium screen, tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

üîß Troubleshooting

My recipes aren't loading
Make sure you have an active internet connection. Try pulling down to refresh. If the problem persists, close the app completely and reopen it.
I lost my saved recipes
If you're logged in, your recipes sync across devices. Try signing out and back in to refresh your data. If you weren't logged in, recipes are stored locally and may be lost if you reinstall the app.
The app is running slowly
Try closing other apps running in the background. Make sure your device has the latest iOS update. If the issue persists, try uninstalling and reinstalling MealSwipe.
