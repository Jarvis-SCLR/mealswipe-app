The short version: We collect minimal data to make MealSwipe work. We don't sell your data. We don't share it with advertisers. Your recipes and meal plans stay private.

1. Information We Collect

When you use MealSwipe, we collect:

  • Account Information: Email address and name when you create an account
  • Recipe Preferences: Your saved recipes, meal plans, and dietary preferences
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app (swipes, features used) to improve our service
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for troubleshooting

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve MealSwipe's features
  • Sync your recipes and meal plans across devices
  • Send important updates about your account or subscription
  • Personalize recipe recommendations (if enabled)
  • Process payments through our secure payment provider (Stripe)

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use:

  • Supabase: For secure user authentication and data storage
  • Stripe: For payment processing (we never see your full card number)

Data is stored on servers in the United States.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with:

  • Service Providers: Companies that help us run MealSwipe (hosting, payments)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your saved recipes and meal plans
  • Opt-out: Disable personalized recommendations in settings

6. Children's Privacy

MealSwipe is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Third-Party Services

MealSwipe uses these third-party services:

  • Spoonacular API: Recipe data and images
  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Supabase: Authentication and database
  • Expo/EAS: App updates and notifications

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights including:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights
