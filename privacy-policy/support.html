Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save a recipe?

Simply swipe right on any recipe card you like, or tap the heart icon. The recipe will be added to your saved recipes.

How do I create a meal plan?

Go to the Planner tab and drag your saved recipes into the day/meal slots. You can plan breakfast, lunch, and dinner for each day of the week.

Can I filter recipes by dietary needs?

Yes! Go to Profile > Dietary Preferences to set your preferences (vegetarian, keto, gluten-free, etc.) and allergies. We'll only show you recipes that match.

How does the grocery list work?

Once you've added recipes to your meal plan, the app automatically generates a shopping list with all the ingredients you'll need, organized by store section.

Can I export my grocery list?

Yes! Tap the share icon on your grocery list to export it to Apple Reminders, Notes, or share via text/email.
