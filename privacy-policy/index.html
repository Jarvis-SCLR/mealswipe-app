Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

Overview

MealSwipe ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Account information (email, name) if you create an account
  • Dietary preferences and allergies you set
  • Recipes you save and meal plans you create

Automatically collected:

  • Device information (type, OS version)
  • App usage analytics (features used, session duration)

How We Use Your Information

  • To personalize recipe recommendations based on your preferences
  • To sync your data across devices (if you create an account)
  • To improve our app and develop new features
  • To send important notifications about your meal plans

Data Storage

Your preferences and saved recipes are stored locally on your device. If you create an account, data is securely stored in our cloud infrastructure to enable sync across devices.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In: For account authentication
  • Stripe: For secure payment processing (we never see your full card details)
  • Analytics: To understand app usage and improve the experience

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with:

  • Service providers who help us operate the app
  • When required by law

Your Rights

You can:

  • Access your data through the app settings
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your recipes and meal plans
  • Opt out of analytics collection

Children's Privacy

MealSwipe is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
