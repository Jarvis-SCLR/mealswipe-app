## Description
Tired of the daily "what's for dinner?" struggle? MealSwipe makes meal planning fun with a Tinder-style swiping interface.

**SWIPE TO DISCOVER**
Browse delicious recipes with a simple swipe. Like what you see? Swipe right to save it. Not your taste? Swipe left and move on. It's that easy.

**PERSONALIZED FOR YOU**
Set your dietary preferences (vegetarian, keto, gluten-free, and more), list allergies, and tell us what appliances you have. We'll only show recipes that work for your kitchen and lifestyle.

**PLAN YOUR WEEK**
Drag your saved recipes into a weekly meal planner. Breakfast, lunch, dinner – all organized in one beautiful calendar view.

**SMART GROCERY LISTS**
Automatically generate shopping lists from your meal plan. Items are organized by store section so you can shop efficiently.

**FEATURES**
• Swipe-based recipe discovery
• 8 dietary preference filters
• Allergy-safe recommendations  
• Weekly meal planning calendar
• Auto-generated grocery lists
• Export lists to Reminders & Notes
• Cooking time filters
• Kitchen appliance matching
• Beautiful recipe cards with photos

Stop dreading meal planning. Start swiping.
