## My Recommendation: Option C (Hybrid)

### Why Hybrid?
1. **One-time option** appeals to subscription-fatigued users
2. **Subscription** provides recurring revenue for active users
3. **Free tier** drives growth and trials
4. **Psychology**: "Own it forever" is powerful messaging

### Pricing Rationale
- **$3.99/mo** is under the $5 mental threshold
- **$39.99/yr** = ~17% discount (standard)
- **$14.99 lifetime** = 4 months of subscription value
- **Business at $14.99/mo** undercuts competitors
